Intublade

The World's Premier Video Laryngoscope


Watch video

Battlefield Inspired, Physician Created

Intublade® is a single-use disposable video laryngoscopy system designed for difficult airways where video visualization of an airway may be compromised due to bodily fluids.

Through a patented lens cleaning nozzle, saline can be delivered via a leur-lock syringe directly onto the camera lens by the operator.

Meet our Team

Andrew Napier
CEO/Founder

Dr. Napier is an Emergency Medicine physician and innovator. His path into the medical field began in the Army where he was a Combat Medic and was awarded the Purple Heart while deployed to Afghanistan. He invented the device Intublade® and founded the company Bear Creek Medical in order to bring new medical devices into the mainstream market.

Dave Warden III
COO/Co-Founder

Since exiting the military, Mr. Warden has transferred his leadership abilities to help launch multiple successful companies in the private sector. With his proven track record, he was selected for Tampa Bay Business Journal’s: Top 30 Under 30 Business Professionals.
